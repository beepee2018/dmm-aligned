Two Types of Adoption
The Adoption dimension distinguishes foundation modules from expansion modules — because they mean different things.
Foundation Modules
Every dealer goes live with these at cutover. They run the store. Adoption is not optional — it is the starting line.
  • Accounting
  • Parts
  • Service
The signal here is depth, not presence. Are staff genuinely operating in these modules, or just complying?
Expansion Modules
These require a deliberate decision, an internal champion, and real behavioral change. When a dealer activates one, it means something.
  • CRM
  • TekionPay
  • Analytics
  • AI & Predictive Tools
Expansion module activation is the primary adoption signal at Tier 3 and above.
