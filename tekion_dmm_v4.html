Understanding Platform Adoption
Every dealer goes live with Accounting, Parts, and Service. What matters is how far they go from there.
Go-Live Modules
All dealers launch with these modules at cutover. They are the operational foundation — not optional, and not a differentiator. The meaningful signal is depth of use, not presence.
  • Accounting
  • Parts
  • Service
Going live is the starting line, not the finish line. The question at Tier 1 is whether staff are genuinely operating in these modules — or just complying.
Expansion Modules
These represent a deliberate choice — an internal champion, a business case, and real behavioral change. Activation signals meaningful maturity progress and is the primary driver of Tier 3 and Tier 4 advancement.
  • CRM
  • TekionPay
  • Analytics
  • AI & Predictive Tools
Expansion module activation is the clearest adoption signal beyond go-live. When a dealer activates one, something real happened.
CVA Adoption Snapshot
Your CVA brings an adoption snapshot to every first dealer conversation — documenting module status, depth signals, and expansion pipeline across exactly these two categories. The snapshot is what anchors the Customer Value Plan in platform evidence rather than assumption.
